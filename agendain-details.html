App Description

An application for collaboratively determining the agenda together, thereby enhancing the quality and productivity of meetings.

Problems

Effective and efficient meetings are crucial for successful event planning, especially for college students organizing events. Research indicates that many students face challenges in conducting meetings that meet the criteria of effectiveness, efficiency, and value. Meeting effectiveness is measured by the satisfaction of requests, efficiency by the correct handling of items per minute, and the value index emphasizes the importance of agenda topics. One common issue is the selection of agenda items, often determined by a moderator or PIC without participant input, leading to dependency on one person, potential omission of important items, and inclusion of irrelevant ones. To address this, the Vickrey-Clarke-Groves (VCG) method is proposed, encouraging collaborative agenda creation through a scoring system, ensuring each participant's involvement and shared responsibility. This approach aims to enhance the quality of meetings, making them more effective and efficient by prioritizing the most relevant discussion topics.
