App Description

PintarHitung is an app focuses in helping sellers of SME's business to determine the minimum price required to cover their cost without loss by calculating the break-even point for their goods

Problems

Sellers often encounter challenges, primarily due to frequent miscalculations, especially when dealing with bulk product purchases in Small and Medium Enterprises (SMEs). SMEs often buy products in large quantities and sell them in different units – be it per box, per unit, or per piece – each with varying prices. This complexity creates confusion for sellers, particularly when trying to determine their profits and Break Even Points (BEP).

For example, imagine a seller buys 20 boxes of tissue rolls, each containing 10 pieces, spending a total of 3 million rupiah. Now, if they plan to sell at 170k per box and 20k per roll, figuring out how many units need to be sold to reach the Break Even Point becomes a significant challenge. This confusion highlights the pressing need for a solution that simplifies calculations, helping sellers confidently reach their Break Even Points and avoid potential losses.
